We accept crypto (BTC / ETH / BUSD / USDT) or fiat via wallet or bank transfer. You will receive an invoice with your chosen marketing package.

It all depends on your project’s roadmap and its objectives. Fill in the Brief Form here to help us understand your project and an account manager will contact you in 48 hours.

No, none of the marketing packages have any advertising (media) budget included. In all the marketing packages, the performance ads budget includes media planning, campaigns implementation, tracking, optimization and reporting.
